How It Works

Meal Swipes

Use meal swipes for all-you-care-to-eat dining at our main halls. Each swipe gives you unlimited access during that meal period.

Dining Dollars

Dining Dollars work like cash at all campus dining locations. Perfect for grab-and-go meals, snacks, and retail dining options.

Flexibility

Mix and match meal swipes and Dining Dollars based on your schedule. Perfect for busy students with varying dining needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do I need to select my meal plan?

Meal plan selection is required during housing registration for on-campus students. Changes can be made during the first two weeks of each semester.

Can I change my meal plan during the semester?

Yes, meal plan changes are allowed during the first two weeks of each semester. After this period, only upgrades are permitted.

What are Dining Dollars?

Dining Dollars are part of your meal plan that can be used at any campus dining location. They work like cash and don't expire until the end of the academic year.

Do unused meal swipes roll over?

Some plans allow limited rollover of unused meals within the same week. Meals do not carry over between weeks or semesters.

Can I use my meal plan for guests?

Most meal plans include guest privileges, allowing you to treat friends and family. Guest meal costs are deducted from your Dining Dollars.
