Dietary Accommodations

Common Accommodations:

Gluten-Free Allergen-Free Religious Medical
  • Celiac disease and gluten sensitivity
  • Nut, dairy, egg, and other allergies
  • Kosher, Halal, and other dietary laws
  • Diabetes, eating disorders, and medical diets

How to Get Started:

  1. Contact our Dining Services office.
  2. Schedule a consultation with our dietitian.
  3. Provide medical documentation if required.
  4. Receive your personalized dining plan.
